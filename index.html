Financial institutions

We help banks, investment firms, and other financial institutions to embed central bank digital currencies, programmable money, and other tokenised assets into their businesses.

Enterprises

Companies of all sizes, from multinationals to SMEs, are building new business models on blockchain. We can get you to market faster, using technology thats ready for the future.

Partners

Whether youre a vendor or a consultant, chances are your clients are drawn to the benefits of blockchain but daunted by the specialist knowledge required. We help you, help them.

Developers

You may not be part of the 1% of developers who understands the complexities of blockchain. With Quant Connect, you dont need to be to build powerful apps that will run on any chain.

Why Quant?

We make blockchain simple, trusted, and future-proof.

Interoperability

We believe the future of finance will be built on interconnected networks with value in the form of smart money, tokenised assets, and information flowing  freely between them.
Ease of use

Overledger, our platform that underpins some of the most demanding blockchain-based use cases, was designed to be used without specialist knowledge.
Security

Our founder and CEO has more than 20 years of cybersecurity experience and C-level accountability as a CISO, CIO and CTO. He has worked for government as well as the private sector and ensures that, at Quant, security is job one.
Resilience

We engineer resilience into our technology because we believe it is as much about enabling upside as it is protecting against risk. Resilience has its own value, providing opportunity in a volatile world.
Programmability

One of the most powerful aspects of tokenised money is the ability to embed gradual control of its behaviour. The money of the future will be smarter, more useful, more purposeful.
Adaptability

Our technology makes it easy to deploy new tokens, to move them from chain to chain, to adapt smart contracts to new use cases. Because your choices today shouldnt hold you back tomorrow.
